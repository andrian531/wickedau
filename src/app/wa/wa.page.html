Start your roadtrip from Western Australia

If you are starting your roadtrip from Western Australia, feel free to pickup and drop-off your van from our depot either in Perth, Broome or Exmouth. From Broome in the rugged Kimberly region, there is a vast rugged mountains if you head north. And to the south, you can find amazing sandy beaches like Eighty Mile Beach. Or start from Exmouth where is all about the COAST and REEF like The Pinnacles, Kalbarri National Park, Shark Bay, Monkey Mia, Ningaloo Reef and Coral Bay.

The West Coast of Australia has got tons of untouched coast. You can enjoy the spectacular red rock gorges and rugged coastal cliffs at Kalbarri National Park, snorkel Ningaloo Reef, swin with whale sharks in the season or spot humpbacks from the beach. Then head to Karijini National Park for your ideal Outback adventure.

Experience the unique untouched coast of the west. Coral Bay, Exmouth, Ningaloo Reef, Kalbarri and Karijini National Parks and more on a Wicked campervan. Take off on an adventure and head for the open road in one of our well furnished, clean, comfortable campervans. Our campervans, 4wd and car rental come fully equipped with everything you need to make your holiday a wonderful experience at a cheap price. The pure freedom of self-drive gives you the independence to tour wherever you like at your leisure.
